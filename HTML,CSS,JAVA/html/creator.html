Sidemen

The Sidemen are a popular British YouTube collective consisting of seven members: KSI, Miniminter, TBJZL, Vikkstar123, W2S, Behzinga, and Zerkaa. They are known for producing a variety of content, including challenges, sketches, and gaming videos, and have a combined total of over 155 million subscribers across their channels as of May 2025. You can find their videos on their official YouTube channel, where they post new content every Sunday.
